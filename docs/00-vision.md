# 00 — Vision & Strategy

## What FluenSys is

Fluensys Limited is an independent engineering consultancy in Central
London with 50+ years of combined pump & fluids expertise, serving heavy
industry and SMEs across water, oil & gas, chemical processing, power and
renewables. Three service pillars: **INNOVATE** (new product development,
hydraulic design, CFD/FEA), **OPTIMIZE** (troubleshooting, debottlenecking,
energy-intensity reduction), **MODERNIZE** (capital projects, equipment
selection, Net Zero alignment).

## What this platform is for

The v1 site (fluensys-site) was a competent brochure with a small blog and
an email-provider subscription form. v2 is a **platform**:

1. **Authority engine.** The Journal grows to thousands of enriched
   articles — each with figures, interactive charts and a downloadable PDF
   — authored by consultants and by AI routines they commission and review.
   Depth + rigor + presentation = the reference library for pumping
   systems.
2. **Traffic engine.** Technical long-tail SEO ("cryogenic pump seal
   failure", "VFD energy savings system curve") is the channel. Every page
   is static, fast, structured-data-rich and shareable.
3. **Lead engine.** Readers convert: newsletter subscription (Firestore,
   topic-segmented), service enquiries (every pillar links to a request),
   and a reader base that returns (bookmarks, comments, guest→Google
   account upgrade path).

## Positioning & voice

- **Authoritative, not academic.** Field engineers writing for field
  engineers. Case studies with real numbers beat theory.
- **Practical, tailor-made, honest** — the brand values (Honesty,
  Integrity, Responsibility) show up as: cite sources, state limits of
  applicability (per-article disclaimers), no hype.
- **British English**, metric-first with imperial where the industry uses
  it.

## Experience principles

1. **Exquisite restraint.** One WebGL statement (hero flow field), surgical
   GSAP reveals, generous whitespace. The wow factor is coherence, not
   noise.
2. **Native-like on everything.** 60fps transforms only, reduced-motion and
   save-data fallbacks, touch-first nav, no layout shift, instant
   navigation (SSG).
3. **Reading is the product.** Article typography, TOC, progress, figures
   and charts get the most design attention of anything on the site.

## Success metrics (review quarterly)

- Organic search impressions/clicks for non-brand pump queries
- Newsletter subscriber count + topic distribution
- PDF downloads per article
- Returning readers (bookmark/comment activity)
- Service enquiries attributable to articles
